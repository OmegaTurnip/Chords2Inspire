Charles - Keina Suda

A musical analysis. [FULL GUITAR TAB]April 7, 2014

The hit J-Pop single by Keina 'Balloon' Suda took the internet music scene by storm when it was released in 2017, racking up to 84 million views on Youtube and becoming one of the most famous Vocaloid songs in history. From becoming a staple song in most typical karaoke sessions, to being covered by thousands of singers on the internet, you might wonder about what makes this song so catchy? Well lucky for you, I'm here to break it down, and analyse why this song has become a timeless masterpiece.

Hi, I'm Ludoo, and welcome to my blog!

Chords2Inspire is a blog website for musicians who like making and reading about music. On the one side, you can read posts of me analysing my favorite music and seeing what makes them so catchy! On the other side, I've developed tools for musicians that can help them get inspired through things such as sound synthesis and making chord progressions. This website is still in development, however any constructive feedback is welcomed. Just email me at stludoo6@gmail.com.
